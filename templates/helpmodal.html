Help 幫助
*
asterisk | match zero or more words
[ play * role ] --> [ play a role ] and [ play an important role ]
_
understroke | match any word
[ listen _ music ] --> [ listen to music ]
~ TERM
tilde | search for the similar words of TERM
[ ~reliable person ] --> [ trustworthy person ]
? TERM
question mark | search for TERM optionally
[ listen ?to music ] --> [ listen to music ] or [ discuss ?prep. it ] --> [ discuss it ]
TERM1 / TERM2
slash | either TERM1 or TERM2
[ go in/to school ] --> [ go to school ]
pos
part-of-speech | search for word with specific pos tag
[ v. a report ] --> [ submit a report ]

v. (verb) n. (noun) adj. (adjective) adv. (adverb) prep. (preposition) det. (determiner) conj. (conjunction) pron. (pronoun)

remember to keep a dot(.) after each pos tag
NOTE
the maximum number of words in a query is 5
*
星號 | 查詢任意零到多個詞
[ play * role ] --> [ play a role ] 及 [ play an important role ]
_
底線 | 查詢任意一個詞
[ listen _ music ] --> [ listen to music ]
~ TERM
波浪號 | 查詢同義詞
[ ~reliable person ] --> [ trustworthy person ]
? TERM
問號 | 查詢是否需要特定詞
[ listen ?to music ] --> [ listen to music ] 或 [ discuss ?prep. it ] --> [ discuss it ]
TERM1 / TERM2
斜線 | 查詢何者較佳
[ go in/to school ] --> [ go to school ]
pos
詞性 | 使用詞性查詢任意詞
[ v. a report ] --> [ submit a report ]

v. (動詞) n. (名詞) adj. (形容詞) adv. (副詞) prep. (介系詞) det. (冠詞) conj. (連接詞) pron. (代名詞)
